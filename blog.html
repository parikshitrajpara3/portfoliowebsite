Paul Graham's Writing Advice

Writing is thinking, but the converse is not true.

PG’s recent essay mentions the idea on how writing would become obsolete because of tools like ChatGPT.

Because of the AI, the world will be divided into people who can write and those who cannot.

He mentions that writing is an extension of a certain type of thinking.

Writing allows people to remove all the biases and fuzziness, making ideas clearer.

And the world will be filled with people who can think and those who cannot.

OpenAI Co-founder’s advice to Machine Learning Beginners

If you are a beginner, it often seems daunting to know how many skills you need to learn to become an engineer.

Therefore, to avoid wasting time during a process that is already so complicated, it is better to focus on finding the right resources and best tools in the market before you start your journey.

However, according to Andrej Karpathy, this is awful advice.

He mentions that the focus should be more on doing a huge volume of work rather than finding the ideal course or perfect tool.

This is because once you begin your journey you will start making many errors which are crucial.

All those errors will act as scar tissue that will help you become better at your skill and later help you navigate efficiently.

The focus should be more on the quantity of work rather than quality for beginners.
